Boba Optimization

Boba Calculator to optimize sales by minimizing the amount of production cost.

Ingredient and Recipe Table

Firstly, enter your boba variants in the "Enter product name" section and click "add product recipe to list. After that, enter all the required information. On the recipe column, you can select the appropriate product and the amount of the ingredient it uses in the "product unit" section. If the product unit is in milliliters (ml), then the supply should also be milliliters (ml).
Eg. Milk Tea would use up 200 ml of milk. The ingredient would be milk, while the product is Milk Tea, and the product unit is 200.
